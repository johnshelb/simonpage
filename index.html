Welcome to WebSimon!

Here's how to play

  • Choose your setting:
    • Select the number of elements you want, or choose your own length (Custom)
    • Select the speed, or choose your own speed (Custom)

  • Click "Show Pattern"
  • Pay attention! You will see a sequence of colors
  • When the sequence is finished, click on the colors in the correct order

    • Scoring

  • If you get it right on the first try, you win points! (points earned = number of elements/time)
  • If you get it right after the first try, you earn 1 point!
  • If you need a hint, click "Show it again" (you'll lose 1/2 a point each time you do though!)
  • Higher scores are awarded for longer sequences and for shorter times
  • Try to get the highest score of all your friends!
